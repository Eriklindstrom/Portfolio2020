House Next Door

Home Projects Project

A solo project in which I took on the task of creating a photorealistic 3D render of the KTH courtyard in Unreal Engine. I worked in Maya to create the meshes using textures taken from camera photographs. The project features a day and night cycle, custom skybox, custom sounds, as well as a snow particle system. The particle system interacts with the ground and gradually fills in the cracks of the stonework with snow.
